About Us

Every member of the Eudaimonia community may use the platform without the hassle of agreeing to strict guidelines. This is a free platform hosted by students with the primary motive of learning and providing a peaceful environment for personal development and growth that may not be otherwise available elsewhere on the web.

Although there exists no pressure of going through any Terms and Conditions, we are still providing the link for those interested.


Utilities

The complete set of utilities that we provide:

  • A personalised Notebook for the users to use without any limit (See Limits for more information).
  • Allowing the use of images as a part of the entry (See Limits for more information).
  • Smart-recovery of deleted entries (See Storage for more information).
  • Encryption of all entries by Military Grade 256-bit AES algorithm (See Security for more information).

Storage

Although Eudaimonia in itself does not impose any limit on the utilization of any of its sources, the development team is bounded by technicalities imposed by their implementations (See Limits for more information).

Eudaimonia allows the feature of Smart-recovery of all entries on the platform. But it is of paramount importance to know that over-utilization of the feature is greatly discouraged primarily for two reasons:

  • Data Recovery techniques are expensive and create a considerable overhead on the resources.
  • Frequent deletions and recovery defeats the primal purpose of journaling, which is the creation of a preservable record of reference, for whatsoever purpose the user may see fit, that is not to be deleted in the first place, unless very sure of the reasons of its defragmentation.

It is our duty to instill proper sense of the art to journaling, so that the users may benefit from the activity to the maximum extent. Therefore, even though we provide the facilities, deletions and recovery are not encouraged at all.

The implementation of the data-recovery technique used for the application shall not be disclosed.


Security

Eudaimonia is supported by web-frameworks that put security above all else. Amidst the many vulnarabilities all over the web, care is taken to secure all components of the user data. Technicalities to the said statement are explained as:

  • The source code of the application does not reveal any configuration variable or secret values that any attacker may leverage to gain access to discreet information (sensitive data have been exported as Environment Variables on the host server).
  • All forms have been protected against SQL Injection attacks.
  • All forms have CSRF token validation.
  • The support for logging in through other socials such as GMail, Facebook, etc. has been prohibited to strictly ensure that the password being set for the Eudaimonia account is unique and different from the rest. This has been done to ensure that anyone else using the same computer would not be able to access the entries. Users are suggested to not let the browser save their credentials.

The Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) is a symmetric block cipher chosen by the U.S. government to protect classified information. AES-256 uses a 256-bit key length to encrypt and decrypt a block of messages and it has the following attributes that make it uncrackable:

  • It is a Quantum-secure encryption standard, which means that even quantum computers would not be unable to crack it because the cipher can adapt to a quantum attack by increasing its key size.
  • The AES security is an impermeable layer of security that exists beneath several layers of protection of the database. Even in extreme cases of data breach of the database (the occurence that does not have any chance of taking place, theoretically), there is no possible mechanism to break the encryption that gets generated.

Hence, the security measures provided are more than sufficient to keep compromise of data in check and provide an unbreakable environment. The only way in which the data of a potential user might leak out is if someone else knows their password (or if someone else sneaks up behind them).

The implementation of the AES Encryption technique used for the application shall not be disclosed.


Limits

Project Eudaimonia has been developed with the sole purpose of learning how to craft a functional and marketable product that is commercially viable and scalable for free. But the project is built on the free tiers of finite resources (in terms of textual data storage, media data storage, hosting hours and caching) that have the ability to be upscaled but would require funds.

Hence, the team of developers at Eudaimonia would like to make the following points clear on the term for which the application may be used:

  • The exhaustion of resources is an unlikely outcome since the site would not gather traffic (again, serving high traffic was not the intention of the developers). Hence the lifetime of the site may be approximated as infinite.
  • In the unlikely scenario of exhaustion of resources, by whatsoever reasons, the service and support would be discontinued with a notice.
  • In the scenario of the team permanently stopping development of the web application, the website shall continue to exist independently with no flaws up until the resource cap is reached. The systems have been built capable of unmaintained existence.

Eudaimonia would like to reiterate the fact that this endeavour was an attempt at allowing people to inculcate journaling into their daily lives. Hence, the possibility of a premature and unforeseen end to the services offered should not discourage them to embark on this journey.
